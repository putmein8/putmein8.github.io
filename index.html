Put me in

Read your own story

PutMeIn is an engine that allows users to customize the names of the people involved in their favorite stories. Our proprietary algorithm identifies the names of characters in your story, and gives you the opportunity to change them. Based on your instructions, it generates an updated story with your very own cast of characters. Try it out!
